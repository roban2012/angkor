泰姬陵（Taj Mahal）
  浏览全部图片

印度的泰姬陵于公元1632年开始兴建，是杰出的穆斯林艺术（深入研究可先阅读西北大学彭树志研究小组的《中东史》）。The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his third wife, Mumtaz Mahal. The Taj Mahal is widely recognized as "the jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage". The Mughal emperors were Muslims and direct descendants of Genghis Khan through Chagatai Khan and Timur.
